Minecraft Enters 'The Copper Age' with Major 1.21.9 Update

Mojang has officially launched the highly anticipated 'Copper Age' game drop, bringing version 1.21.9 to both Java and Bedrock editions. The update introduces a massive collection of copper-based items, including new tools, armor, lighting sources, and the adorable, versatile Copper Golem mob.

EA to be Acquired in Record-Breaking $55 Billion Private Equity Buyout

Electronic Arts (EA), the publisher behind huge franchises like Madden NFL, The Sims, and Battlefield, has agreed to be acquired by an investor group in a massive deal valued at $55 billion. This marks one of the largest private equity buyouts in history and is expected to close in early 2027.
